compareReasonable: (a: T, b: T) => ComparatorValue = makeOrderedComparator<string>([compareEmpty,comparePunctuationAndOther,compareStringsWithEmoji,(a, b) => compareReasonableText(makeReasonable(a), makeReasonable(b)),])
  • Defined in src/util/compareThought.ts:127

A comparator that compares by reasonable, human-readable value:

  1. empty
  2. punctuation (=, +, #hi, =test)
  3. emoji
  4. compareReasonableText on text without emoji
